Trooff

Trooff is a non-profit organization that equipts and supports people living in developing countries. We educate individuals on pottery art and provide them with a platform to reach the masses by selling them online. With the income, the individulas are able to purchase seeds and animals to farm and sustain their livelihood. The families also receive basic education on finance, health and farming to encourage growth and success.

People living in third world countries like Cambodia, India and Thailand struggle to live. The children are malnoutrishioned and parents are barely scraping by. Many are fortunate to even afford 1 meal a day and often go to bed hungry. This is a sad reality that will be passed down for many generations to come if help is not provided.

Troff makes relationships and works with local villages to educate individuals with pottery making. We have experts who are able to volunteer in teaching and guiding the villagers to create professional pieces. When the artworks are completed, Troff provides an online platform where pieces can be sold globally. All donations and profits received is given back to the villages and individuals so that they can provide for their families.
